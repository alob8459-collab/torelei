// Risk Management

Built to keep
you in the game.

Protecting capital is the first rule. Every layer of risk management runs automatically — you never have to think about it.

01

2% Risk Per Trade

Position size is calculated based on your exact SL distance and account balance. Never risk more than 2% on any single trade.

02

Breakeven Protection

When a trade reaches 1:1.5 RR, the stop loss moves to entry automatically. The remaining position runs to 1:2 RR with zero risk.

03

Daily Loss Limit

If the daily loss limit is hit, the bot stops trading for the rest of the day. No revenge trading, no accumulating losses.

04

Daily & Weekly Profit Goals

When profit targets are reached, the bot stops. Gains are locked in — no giving back profits by overtrading.

05

Max 3 Concurrent Trades

The bot never opens more than 3 positions at once, keeping total portfolio heat under control at all times.

// FAQ

Common questions.


Can you withdraw money from my account?
No. Your broker requires identity verification for any withdrawal. We only have trading access — we can open and close trades, but cannot move funds out of your account.
Which brokers are supported?
Any broker that uses MetaTrader 5. Weltrade, ICMarkets, XM, Pepperstone, FBS, Exness, and hundreds more. We automatically detect your broker's exact symbol names during setup.
How long does setup take?
After payment, our team contacts you within 24 hours during working days and market hours (Mon–Fri, 08:00–20:00 UTC). The setup itself takes around 15–30 minutes and you'll be live the same day.
What happens if I want to stop?
You can pause or stop trading anytime from the Telegram control panel. You can also disconnect your account completely with one tap. Your lifetime access remains — reconnect anytime.
What is the minimum account size?
We recommend a minimum of $500 to allow proper position sizing at 2% risk per trade. The bot automatically adapts lot sizes to your account balance.
Is this a managed account service?
TORELEI is an automated copy trading tool. You remain the full account owner and retain control at all times. You can revoke access or pause trading instantly from the Telegram bot.
